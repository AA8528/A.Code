2 Cool 4 Skool

2 Cool 4 Skool is the debut single album released by South Korean boy band BTS on June 12, 2013 through Big Hit Entertainment and distributed by Loen.
The album was supported by two singles "No More Dream" and "We Are Bulletproof Pt.2". Commercially, it peaked at number five on the Gaon Album Chart in South Korea, and at number ten on Billboard's World Albums Chart in the United States.

The music video for "No More Dream" was released on June 12, 2013 ahead of BTS debut showcase.
"No More Dream" dance version was later released on June 16, 2013
