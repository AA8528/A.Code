Map of the Soul 7

Map of the Soul: 7 is the fourth Korean-language (seventh overall) studio album by South Korean boy band BTS.
The album was released on February 21, 2020, by Big Hit Entertainment.
It is the follow-up to their 2019 extended play Map of the Soul: Persona, with five of its songs appearing on the album.
For the album, BTS worked with Halsey, Ed Sheeran, Troye Sivan and Sia.
The album was supported by two singles, both entering the US Billboard Hot 100: "Black Swan" debuted and peaked at number 57, while "On" reached number 4, becoming the band's first top-five hit on the chart.
